Project Practices

Transcriptions are produced according to the conventions of philological editing of medieval texts. The Junicode typeface is used for transcription texts to ensure correct display of medieval characters (medieval Unicode, letters with special diacritics, abbreviations, etc.).

Additional Resources

Manuscript metadata is drawn from the Jonas catalogue (Institut de recherche et d'histoire des textes – CNRS). The transcription typeface is Junicode (Peter S. Baker), distributed under the Open Font License. The main reading typeface is Atkinson Hyperlegible, developed by the Braille Institute of America.
