Objectives

This project aims to produce digital editions of saints' Lives in Old French (langue d'oïl) preserved in medieval manuscripts, organized by saint and by manuscript. Bibliographic metadata is drawn from the Jonas (IRHT–CNRS) catalogue and updated automatically.

Editorial Principles

Transcriptions are produced according to the conventions of philological editing of medieval texts. The Junicode typeface is used for the rendering of transcription texts in order to ensure correct display of medieval characters (medieval Unicode, letters with special diacritics, abbreviations, etc.).

Sources and Acknowledgments

Manuscript metadata is drawn from the Jonas catalogue (Institut de recherche et d'histoire des textes – CNRS), freely available online. We thank the IRHT for making this data available.

The typeface used for transcriptions is Junicode (Peter S. Baker), distributed under the Open Font License.

The main reading typeface is Atkinson Hyperlegible, developed by the Braille Institute of America to improve legibility.
